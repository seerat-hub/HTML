CPU scheduling

1. what is cpu scheduling?

CPU scheduling is the process of switching between processes that are being executed on a computer's central processing unit (CPU)

types

  1. First Come First Serve FCFS
  2. Shortest Job First SJF
  3. Priority CPU Scheduling PCS
  4. Round Robin SchedulingRRS
